PSG College of Technology

PSG College of Technology, established in 1951, is one of the premier engineering institutions in India. Located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, the college is known for its excellence in technical education and research.

The Department of Mathematics at PSG College of Technology has been at the forefront of mathematical research and education for over seven decades. The department is well-equipped with modern facilities and experienced faculty.

  • High-speed Wi-Fi throughout campus
  • Ample parking facilities
  • Multiple dining options
  • Guest house accommodation
  • 45 minutes from Coimbatore Airport
  • 30 minutes from Coimbatore Railway Station
