About ICAMM 2025

The Sixth International Conference on Applied Mathematical Models (ICAMM 2025) will be held in the Department of Mathematics at PSG College of Technology, Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. This prestigious conference brings together researchers, academicians, and industry professionals from around the world to share their latest findings and innovations in applied mathematics.

PSG College of Technology, established in 1951 by PSG & Sons' Charities, is an ISO 9001-2015 certified autonomous college affiliated to Anna University. The college has been at the forefront of technical education and research for over seven decades.

  • Language: English
  • Peer review process will be followed
